## -description

The **WIFI_ADAPTER_TYPE** enumeration specifies the Wi-Fi adapter type.

Extensible station. 

### -field WIFI_ADAPTER_WIFI_DIRECT_ROLE

Wi-Fi Direct Role.  

### -field WIFI_ADAPTER_SECONDARY_STATION
