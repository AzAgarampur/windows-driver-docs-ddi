## -description

The **D3D12DDI_VIDEO_ENCODER_PROFILE_HEVC_0080** enumerator contains a list of possible HEVC profiles.

## -enum-fields

### -field D3D12DDI_VIDEO_ENCODER_PROFILE_HEVC_0080_MAIN

The HEVC Main profile.

### -field D3D12DDI_VIDEO_ENCODER_PROFILE_HEVC_0080_MAIN10

The HEVC Main 10 profile.
